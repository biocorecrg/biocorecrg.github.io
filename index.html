CRG Bioinformatics core facility

Welcome to the CRG Bioinformatics Core!
We provide researchers with services of analyses of high-throughput sequencing (NGS, Nanopore, droplet-based) and other biomedical data, software/pipelines and database development, and bioinformatics training.
We focus on delivering reproducible results using the modern computational solutions (NextFlow, Cloud, Linux containers).
